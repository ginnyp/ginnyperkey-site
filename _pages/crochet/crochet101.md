## <a name="whats-next?"></a>What's Next?

- **Practice your skills** by finishing your project using the steps and resources below  
- **Join a local crafting circle or crochet group** to connect with others (there's one every week at the Greenpoint library!)
- **Keep learning** by signing up for additional crochet workshops at Brooklyn Craft Company, such as:
    - [**Crochet 101: Chunky Cowl**](https://www.brooklyncraftcompany.com/collections/knitting-workshops/products/crochet-101): Learn the double crochet stitch and how to work in the round.
    - [**Crochet 101: Lil' Scarf**](https://www.brooklyncraftcompany.com/collections/knitting-workshops/products/crochet-101-cozy-tie-scarf): Learn the single crochet stitch, crochet decreases, and how to work in rows.
    - [**Crochet 102: Bandana**](https://www.brooklyncraftcompany.com/collections/knitting-workshops/products/crochet-101-bandana-1): Learn how to shape crochet fabric by using increase and decreases.
    - [**Tunisian Crochet 101**](https://www.brooklyncraftcompany.com/collections/knitting-workshops/products/tunisian-crochet-101): Learn Tunisian crochet basics and make an ear warmer.
    - [**Crochet Hexagon Motifs**](https://www.brooklyncraftcompany.com/products/crochet-hexagon-motifs): Learn the double crochet stitch, how to make hexagon motifs, and the zipper method for seaming.
    - [**Upcycled Crochet Rag Rug**](https://www.brooklyncraftcompany.com/products/diy-rag-rug): Learn how to make your own yarn out of fabric scraps and use it to crochet a circular rug.
    - [**Crochet a Summer Top**](https://www.brooklyncraftcompany.com/collections/knitting-workshops/products/crochet-a-summer-tank): Learn new stitch patterns and techniques for making crochet clothes.

- **Bookmark this page** so you can revisit links as you need them  
- **Join [Ravelry](https://www.ravelry.com/)** and start finding patterns you are excited to make!
