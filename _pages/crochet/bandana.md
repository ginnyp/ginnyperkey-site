## Pattern Summary – with video links!

**Getting Started**

1. Make a slip knot  
   - [Slip knot tutorial](https://www.woolandthegang.com/en/tutorial/how-to-make-a-slip-knot)
2. Chain (ch) 2  
   - [Chain stitch tutorial](https://www.woolandthegang.com/en/tutorial/how-to-crochet-chain-stitch)
3. Single crochet (sc) into the first chain (2nd from the hook) [1 stitch]  
   - [Single crochet tutorial](https://www.woolandthegang.com/en/tutorial/how-to-crochet-single-crochet)
4. Ch 1, turn, and do a single crochet increase (2 sc in same stitch) [2 stitches]  
   - [How to crochet increases](https://www.woolandthegang.com/en/tutorial/how-to-increase-in-half-double-crochet) *(Note: shown with a different stitch)*

**Increase to Center**

5. Ch 1, turn, sc across [2 stitches]  
6. Ch 1, turn, 2 sc in first stitch, sc across [3 stitches]  
7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 twenty-seven more times [30 stitches at widest point]  

**Decrease to End**

8. Ch 1, turn, sc until 2 stitches remain, sc2tog (single crochet two together) [29 stitches]  
   - [How to decrease in single crochet](https://www.woolandthegang.com/en/tutorial/how-to-decrease-in-single-crochet)
9. Ch 1, turn, sc across  
10. Repeat steps 8 and 9 twenty-seven more times [2 stitches remain]  
11. Ch 1, turn, sc2tog [1 stitch]  
12. Ch 1, turn, sc [1 stitch]

**Border**

14. Ch 1, then sc around the edges of the bandana, placing 3 sc in the same st at the points of the border.
   - *Note: If using a contrasting color, fasten off main color and attach new yarn.*
   - [How to add a single crochet border](https://www.woolandthegang.com/en/tutorial/how-to-make-a-single-crochet-edge-trim)

**Finishing**

15. Cut the yarn, leaving a 6" tail, and pull the tail through the final loop  
16. Weave in your ends  
   - [How to weave in ends](https://www.woolandthegang.com/en/tutorial/how-to-weave-in-your-ends-in-crochet)
